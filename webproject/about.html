About the TAJ

Theo, Abeer, and Jack are all Londoners who attend University of the Arts London High Holborn campus to study Computer Science. Our speculative interests lead us to predict the future of our campus' neighbourhood, inspired by trends we found through conducting research as well as the local government (Camden Council) being a driving force for eco-friendly environments. Theo is from Warwickshire, and currently. Jack is from Jersey Channel Islands, and had visited London many times before moving to university and has always enjoyed being in the city.
